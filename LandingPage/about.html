Systems Engineering & Automation

About ORBYTH

ORBYTH is a systems engineering and automation firm specialized in logistics operations.

We design, integrate, and secure digital infrastructures for companies operating in warehousing, transport, and supply chain environments. Our work focuses on reducing operational friction by replacing fragmented tools and manual workflows with coherent, scalable systems.

Founded by Souhaib and Ali, ORBYTH was built to solve a structural issue in logistics: operations have evolved faster than the systems supporting them.

What ORBYTH Does

ORBYTH operates at the system level of logistics organizations.

We design and implement:

  • End-to-end workflow automation
  • Integrations between WMS, TMS, ERP, and external platforms
  • Custom internal interfaces and dashboards
  • Secure data flows and access control

Automation is applied selectively — only where it improves reliability, visibility, and control.

"No generic stacks. No rigid frameworks. Every solution is designed around how the operation actually runs."

Built for Logistics Reality

Logistics environments are defined by:

  • High transaction volume
  • Time-sensitive execution
  • Multiple systems and stakeholders
  • Low margin for error

Most inefficiencies are not caused by people, but by disconnected systems and unclear process ownership.

ORBYTH creates structured operational ecosystems where data moves predictably, processes execute consistently, and teams operate with clarity.

Solutions by Operational Role

Tailored approaches for every layer of your logistics organization.

For Warehouse Operations

Warehouse teams deal with constant execution pressure and system fragmentation.

ORBYTH helps warehouse operations by:

  • • Automating inbound, outbound, and internal warehouse workflows
  • • Connecting WMS with planning, reporting, and external systems
  • • Reducing manual data entry and operational handoffs
  • • Providing clear, role-based interfaces for daily execution
The result: fewer errors, faster throughput, and predictable warehouse operations.

For Transport & Dispatch Operations

Transport operations depend on timing, coordination, and accurate data.

ORBYTH supports transport teams by:

  • • Integrating TMS with order, warehouse, and financial systems
  • • Automating dispatch, status updates, and exception handling
  • • Centralizing operational visibility across shipments and partners
  • • Reducing dependency on manual coordination and ad-hoc communication
The result: improved planning accuracy, better service levels, and lower operational stress.

For Management & Decision Makers

Management requires control, visibility, and systems that scale.

ORBYTH delivers for leadership teams by:

  • • Creating real-time operational dashboards and reporting layers
  • • Ensuring data consistency across departments
  • • Building secure, auditable system architectures
  • • Reducing operational risk tied to key individuals or manual processes
The result: informed decision-making and infrastructure that supports growth without increasing complexity.

How ORBYTH Works

ORBYTH does not act as a tool reseller or short-term implementer.

We engage as long-term technical partners and deliver:

  • Clear system architecture
  • Transparent logic and documentation
  • Maintainable solutions that internal teams can own and extend

Our systems are designed to remain stable as operations grow and tools evolve.
